About Us

AEC is a construction company that has built a strong reputation for delivering quality service, on time and at a competitive price. We pride ourselves on being skilled and experienced in all aspects of road maintenance and this, combined with our extensive knowledge and experience in the road maintenance and product manufacturing industries, have established us as major players in this industry.

Our core values

Our commitment to innovation and excellence invariably results in a successfully completed project for both contractor and client. We understand and promote the idea of working as a partnership with our clients to ensure their goals are met. Our unique and flexible project management systems ensure that a positive outcome is achieved regardless of size or nature of the project.

Our Team

Our team is committed to working with clients to ensure that new projects are completed to specifications and that all maintenance work meets with clients requirements. We recognize that every construction is unique in scope, but our process is systemically applied to each project. We take pride in distinguishing ourselves by always doing it with a smile on our faces. We love what we do every day and it shows in our prices and quality of work.
